Rice Krispie Treats

How To Make Rice Krispe Treats

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 Package of marshmallows
  • 6 cups of Rice Krispies cereal

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan melt butter over low heat
  2. Add Rice Krispies cereal, sit until will coated
  3. Using wax paper evenly press mixture into a 13x9x2 inch pan, coated with cooking spray
  4. Allow to cool, then serve
