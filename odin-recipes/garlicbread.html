Garlic Bread

How To Make Garlic Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of Italian or French bread
  • 1/2 cup of unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsely
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat OVen to 350F
  2. Cut the loaf in half, horizontally
  3. Mix the butter, garlic, and parsely together in a small bowl
  4. Spread butter mixture over the two bread halves
  5. Heat in oven on bake for 10 minutes
  6. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese
  7. Slice and serve
